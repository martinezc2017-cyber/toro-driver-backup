Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how
Toro Driver ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal
information as a Driver on our platform.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE:

- Vehicle information (make, model, year, registration, insurance)

- Background check consent and information
- Profile photo

B. INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY:
- Location data (GPS tracking during active rides)
- Trip data (routes, duration, distance)
- Device information (model, OS, unique identifiers)
- App usage data and interaction patterns
- Vehicle telematics (if enabled)

C. INFORMATION FROM THIRD PARTIES:
- Background check results
- Driving record information
- Insurance verification
- Identity verification results

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We use your information to:
- Verify your identity and eligibility
- Connect you with riders seeking services
- Process payments and tax documentation
- Ensure safety and security of our platform
- Improve our services and app experience
- Communicate important updates
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
- Investigate incidents and resolve disputes

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. LOCATION DATA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We collect precise location data to:
- Match you with nearby ride requests
- Provide navigation and routing
- Calculate accurate fares and distances
- Ensure rider safety (share location with riders)
- Investigate incidents and disputes
- Comply with regulatory requirements

Location is tracked when you are online/available.
You can go offline to stop location tracking.

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We may share your information with:

A. RIDERS:
- First name and profile photo

B. SERVICE PROVIDERS:
- Background check companies
- Payment processors
- Insurance verification services
- Cloud hosting providers

C. LEGAL REQUIREMENTS:
- When required by law, subpoena, or court order
- To protect rights, safety, or property
- To investigate potential violations

D. BUSINESS TRANSFERS:
- In connection with merger, acquisition, or sale

WE DO NOT SELL YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. DATA SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Regular security audits and testing
- Access controls and authentication
- Employee training on data protection
- Incident response procedures

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. DATA RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We retain your data for:
- Active accounts: As long as you remain active
- Trip records: 7 years for tax and legal compliance
- Background check data: As required by law
- After deactivation: 90 days, then anonymized/deleted

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You have the right to:
- ACCESS: Request a copy of your personal data
- CORRECT: Update inaccurate information
- DELETE: Request deletion of your account and data
- OPT-OUT: Control marketing communications

DOCUMENTO 4: SAFETY POLICY AGREEMENT
Version 1.0 | Effective Date: January 2025
================================================================================

This Safety Policy Agreement ("Policy") outlines the safety standards and
requirements that all Drivers must follow while using the Toro Driver platform.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Toro Driver maintains a ZERO TOLERANCE policy for the following:

A. SUBSTANCE IMPAIRMENT:
- No driving under the influence of alcohol
- No driving under the influence of drugs (legal or illegal)
- No use of impairing substances while on active duty
- No open containers of alcohol in the vehicle

B. VIOLENCE AND HARASSMENT:
- No physical violence or threats of violence
- No sexual harassment or inappropriate conduct
- No verbal abuse or intimidation
- No discrimination of any kind

C. DANGEROUS DRIVING:
- No excessive speeding (15+ mph over limit)
- No reckless driving or street racing
- No use of mobile devices while driving (except GPS)
- No driving while fatigued beyond safe limits

VIOLATION RESULTS IN IMMEDIATE AND PERMANENT DEACTIVATION.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. VEHICLE SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Before each shift, you must ensure:

A. VEHICLE CONDITION:
- All lights functioning (headlights, brake lights, turn signals)
- Tires properly inflated with adequate tread
- Brakes functioning properly
- Mirrors properly adjusted
- Windshield clean and unobstructed
- Seatbelts functioning for all passengers

B. CLEANLINESS:
- Interior clean and free of debris
- No offensive odors
- Air conditioning/heating functional
- No hazardous materials in vehicle

C. EQUIPMENT:
- First aid kit recommended
- Phone charger available
- Emergency contact information accessible

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. SAFE DRIVING PRACTICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

While providing services, you must:

A. FOLLOW TRAFFIC LAWS:
- Obey all speed limits
- Stop at all stop signs and red lights
- Use turn signals for all lane changes
- Yield to pedestrians
- Maintain safe following distance

B. DEFENSIVE DRIVING:
- Anticipate potential hazards
- Stay alert and focused on the road
- Avoid distractions
- Adjust driving for weather conditions
- Take breaks when fatigued

C. PASSENGER SAFETY:
- Ensure all passengers wear seatbelts
- Wait for passengers to be fully seated before moving
- Assist passengers with accessibility needs
- Never leave children unattended

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. INCIDENT REPORTING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You must immediately report:

- Any accident or collision (no matter how minor)
- Near-miss incidents
- Threatening behavior from riders
- Suspected criminal activity
- Vehicle breakdowns during active trips
- Any safety concerns

Report via: App emergency button, email safety@toro-ride.com, or call support.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. EMERGENCY PROCEDURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

A. IN CASE OF ACCIDENT:
1. Stop immediately and ensure safety
2. Call 911 if injuries or significant damage
3. Exchange information with other parties
4. Document with photos
5. Report to Toro Driver immediately

B. IN CASE OF THREAT:
1. Do not escalate the situation
2. End the trip if safe to do so
3. Drive to a safe, public location
4. Call 911 if immediate danger
5. Report to Toro Driver

C. MEDICAL EMERGENCY:
1. Pull over safely
2. Call 911
3. Provide basic assistance if trained
4. Stay with the passenger until help arrives

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. COVID-19 AND HEALTH SAFETY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

During health emergencies, additional requirements may include:
- Face masks for drivers and passengers
- Regular vehicle sanitization
- Health screening before shifts
- Compliance with local health orders

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. TRAINING AND CERTIFICATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You agree to:
- Complete all required safety training modules
- Maintain any required certifications
- Stay updated on safety policy changes
- Participate in periodic safety reviews

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Safety violations result in:

MINOR VIOLATIONS:
- Warning and required safety refresher training
- Temporary suspension pending review

MAJOR VIOLATIONS:
- Immediate suspension
- Investigation and review
- Possible permanent deactivation

ZERO TOLERANCE VIOLATIONS:
- Immediate and permanent deactivation
- Report to appropriate authorities if applicable

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

By signing this Safety Policy Agreement, I acknowledge that:

- I have read and understand all safety requirements
- I agree to comply with all safety policies
- I understand the consequences of violations
- I will report all safety incidents promptly
- I prioritize the safety of myself, passengers, and the public
- Violation may result in deactivation and legal consequences

================================================================================
END OF SAFETY POLICY AGREEMENT
